A Little About Me

Hi! It's nice to meet you, my name is Connor Saari and I am from Aurora, Colorado. I graduated from Grandview High School, but initially started at Regis Jesuit. During much of my life I played lacrosse, ten years actually. Beyond this, I also played a variety of other sports including water polo and tennis. Sports and athletic activities were a foundational part of my life, and built who I am today. Other things I enjoy are video games, art or graphic design, and cars.
